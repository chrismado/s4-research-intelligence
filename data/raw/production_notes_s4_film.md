**Author:** Chris Matteau
**Date:** June 2024
**Project:** S4 Documentary — Pre-Production Research Phase

## Research Summary

This document compiles key research threads, source assessments, and open questions for the documentary production team.

### Key Claims to Investigate

1. **S4 Facility Existence**: Lazar claims a facility called S4 exists south of Area 51 at Papoose Lake. No government source confirms this specific designation.

2. **Element 115**: Lazar described Element 115 in 1989 — 14 years before it was synthesized. However, the synthesized version (Moscovium, 2003) is wildly unstable (87ms half-life), contradicting Lazar's claim of a stable, handleable isotope.

3. **Educational Background**: Lazar claims MIT and Caltech coursework. No institutional records have been found. His supporters argue records were deliberately erased.

4. **Los Alamos Employment**: Initially denied, then confirmed via phone directory listing. This is the strongest piece of corroborating evidence.

5. **FBI Raid (2017)**: Lazar's business United Nuclear was raided by the FBI. Lazar claims they were searching for Element 115. This is documented by filmmaker Jeremy Corbell.
